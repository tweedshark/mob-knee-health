Mountain Biking Knee Health Routine

Weekly Schedule

  • Monday: MTB Ride (Club) + Light Core (10 min post-ride core + mobility)
  • Tuesday: Mobility + Strength (30-40 min)
  • Wednesday: MTB Ride (Club) + Post-Ride Mobility
  • Thursday: Strength & Stability (30-45 min)
  • Friday: Recovery Walk + Stretching
  • Saturday: Optional Light Plyo & Strength
  • Sunday: Gentle Yoga Flow or Rest

Key Exercises

Glute Bridge

Lie on your back, knees bent, feet flat. Lift hips to form a straight line from shoulders to knees.

Bird-Dog

On all fours, extend opposite arm and leg. Hold 2-3 sec, return, switch sides.

Wall Sit

Lean against a wall, knees at 90 degrees. Hold for 30-60 seconds.

Lateral Band Walks

With band above knees, step sideways while keeping knees aligned over feet.

Hamstring Stretch

Sit with one leg extended, reach towards toes while keeping back straight.

Mobility Routine

Hold each stretch for 30–60 seconds per side:

  • Hamstring stretch
  • Quad stretch
  • Calf stretch
  • Hip flexor stretch
  • IT band stretch
  • Figure-4 glute stretch
  • Child's pose and cat-cow

Tips & Notes

  • Avoid deep squats or lunges past 90 degrees during flare-ups.
  • Skip jumping or plyo moves until strength and control are solid.
  • Track post-ride knee stability on a 1–5 scale.
  • Prioritize recovery—stretch or walk the day after rides.
